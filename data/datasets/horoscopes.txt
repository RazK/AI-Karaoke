MONTHLY COSMIC FORECAST — GENERAL GUIDANCE
This month's dominant energies are shaped by the Saturn return making itself felt across all earth signs, mercury retrograde in its shadow period, and the building tension of a rare venus-mars conjunction that peaks mid-month. All signs will feel the pressure of this configuration, but the outcome depends on the degree of internal clarity each individual brings to the experience.

The north node is asking all of us to move toward our authentic frequency and away from performances of identity that no longer serve our growth. This is not comfortable work. The soul's journey rarely travels through comfort — it travels through the necessary and the true.

The full moon this month carries exceptionally strong manifestation energy. Write down your intentions the night before. Be specific, be honest, and be bold. The universe responds to clarity and courage. Vague wishes produce vague results. Declare what you actually want, not the safe, modest version of it.

Mars in direct motion through Aries is stoking the fire of ambition across all signs. Channel this energy productively — physical movement, creative action, decisive choices. Left unchaneled, this energy becomes agitation and conflict. Choose direction. Choose intention. Choose the path that your highest self would recognize as worthy.

Saturn's discipline and Jupiter's expansion are in a productive but tense square this month, creating a push-pull between caution and leap-of-faith energy. Neither planet is wrong. Both are necessary. The wisdom is in knowing which force to apply at which moment. Meditate. Journal. Listen to your body. Your chakras hold information your mind has not yet processed. Your aura carries signals your consciousness has not yet decoded.

Abundance is not a destination. It is a vibration. Align with it daily through gratitude, intention, and action. The cosmos support you in this. The alignment is near. The transformation is underway. Trust the frequency. Trust the journey. Trust the unfolding.
